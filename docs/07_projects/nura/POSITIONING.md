# NURA Positioning

## 1. Product Definition

NURA is an AI self-understanding product based on Destiny Matrix and Tarot-like
archetypal content. It helps users explore their personal patterns, emotional
landscape, and inner dynamics through a gentle, reflective lens.

Core offering:

```text
Full Destiny Matrix report — one-time purchase
Monthly subscription — daily insights + chat with NURA
```

## 2. Audience

Primary audience:

```text
Mostly women interested in self-understanding, relationships,
personal patterns, emotional clarity, and gentle reflective rituals.
```

They are looking for:

- better understanding of themselves
- insight into relationships and recurring patterns
- a calm, supportive daily practice
- a premium but simple experience

## 3. Value Proposition

NURA offers a soft, premium self-understanding experience.
It helps users see their patterns clearly, without fear, without pressure,
and without deterministic claims.

Key differentiators:

```text
Warm, reflective, not clinical
Archetypal, not occult
Insight-oriented, not predictive
Premium simplicity
```

## 4. Market and Language

```text
Primary market: Russia
Primary language: Russian
Funnel: social media content → landing page → registration / payment / subscription
```

## 5. Commercial Route

```text
social traffic (Telegram, Instagram, VK)
  → landing page (https://nura-ai.ru)
  → registration
  → one-time purchase or subscription
```

Content Plant validation currently uses Telegram as the manual publication
test channel. Real commercial traffic will include other platforms.

## 6. What NURA Is Not

```text
Not therapy
Not fortune telling
Not deterministic prediction
Not an occult promise engine
Not a medical or psychological diagnostic tool
Not a hard sales funnel with fear tactics
Not a "you are broken" framing
```

## 7. Brand Personality

```text
Calm
Warm
Clear
Premium but simple
Reflective
Supportive
Gently wise
Non-judgmental
```
