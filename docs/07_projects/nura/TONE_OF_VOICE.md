# NURA Tone of Voice

## 1. Core Principles

```text
Calm — never rushed, never urgent
Warm — human, approachable, kind
Clear — simple language, no jargon
Premium but simple — elegant, not flashy
Reflective — invites the reader inward
Supportive — encourages, never judges
```

## 2. Allowed Style

```text
Gentle invitations to reflect
Open questions that spark curiosity
Short, clear sentences
Warm and personal address ("you", not "the user")
Insight-oriented framing ("this may help you see...")
Acknowledgment of inner complexity without labeling it as broken
```

## 3. Forbidden Style

```text
Fear-based urgency ("you must act now or...")
Deterministic predictions ("you will meet your partner in June")
Medical or therapeutic language ("diagnose", "treat", "disorder")
Occult promises ("this ritual will change your fate")
Aggressive sales tone ("buy now", "limited offer")
"You are broken" framing ("fix your flaws")
Hard guarantees ("100% guaranteed results")
```

## 4. Allowed Phrases and Examples

```text
"This pattern may show up in your relationships."
"What does this energy invite you to explore?"
"Notice how this resonates within you."
"A gentle invitation to reflect on today's insight."
"Your Destiny Matrix offers a mirror, not a map."
```

## 5. Forbidden Phrases and Examples

```text
"You will definitely attract wealth this month."
"This ritual guarantees a new relationship."
"Your diagnosis according to the Tarot..."
"Fix your broken energy or suffer."
"The cards predict your exact future."
"Limited-time destiny reading — act now!"
```

## 6. Claim Restrictions

```text
No promises of guaranteed outcomes
No claims of predicting the future
No medical claims
No psychological diagnosis claims
No therapeutic claims
No deterministic life forecasts
No manipulation through fear or scarcity
```
